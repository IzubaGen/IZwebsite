SEO Explained: The Invisible Salesman Working 24/7
SEO (Search Engine Optimization) = The process of making your website visible when Rwandans search for products/services like yours on Google.

Local Example: When someone searches:
- "Best coffee shop Kigali" → Bourbon Coffee’s website appears first (not by accident)
- "Buy smartphones Rwanda" → Jumia ranks #1 (earning 50,000+ monthly visits)

Your website could be missing these customers RIGHT NOW.

- Result: 300% more bookings in 4 months
